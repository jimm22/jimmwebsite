Welcome to Bohol

Beautiful beaches, marvelous marine life, and the delightful Chocolate Hills make Bohol the perfect island destination for travelers that want the best of both worlds--adventure and relaxation.

Book a Trip

Natural Beauty

Bohol is home to a wide variety of natural attractions, including the Chocolate Hills, a geological formation of over 1,200 perfectly cone-shaped hills. The island also features beautiful beaches, lush jungles, and crystal-clear waters, making it a perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Cultural Heritage

Bohol has a rich cultural heritage, with many historic sites and landmarks that offer insights into the island's past. Visitors can explore the Baclayon Church, which dates back to the 16th century and is one of the oldest stone churches in the Philippines.

Read More
Ecotourism

Bohol is well known for its ecotourism opportunities, which include wildlife watching and bird watching. The island is home to the Philippine tarsier, the world's smallest primate, as well as many other species of wildlife. There is also Dolphin & Whale Watching, available in certain season.

Read More
Adventure Activities

Bohol offers a wide range of adventure activities for visitors to enjoy, including scuba diving, snorkeling, and island-hopping. Visitors can also take a boat ride to see fireflies and mangrove forest. And for adventure seeker, Canyoneering, Zip-lining and ATV ride to name a few.
